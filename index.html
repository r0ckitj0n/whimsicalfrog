What You’ll Find

Whimsical Frog is built around the idea that everyday spaces should feel a little more playful. You’ll find handmade and small-batch items designed to add warmth and personality to your home, your desk, and the moments you celebrate with friends and family.

Browse the shop to see what’s currently available, or explore themed rooms for seasonal collections and surprise finds. Many items rotate in and out, so if something catches your eye, it’s worth grabbing it while it’s in stock.

Custom Orders

Looking for something specific? We take custom requests when available. If you have an idea, theme, color palette, or occasion in mind, send a note through our contact page and we’ll let you know what’s possible.
